# 82-Factor Model Checklist — Complete Question Bank

Source: 82-Factor Model Checklist

> **Note:** The framework is titled "82 Factor Model" but contains 127 individual questions
> across 82 named factors. Some factors (especially in Legal and Financial) have conditional
> follow-up questions that expand the total count. The "82" refers to the primary factor
> numbering.

---

## 1. Screening

### 1.1 Deal Quality

| #     | Factor                    | Question                                                                         |
| ----- | ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 1.1.1 | Source                    | From what source did the deal come or by whom was the deal referred?             |
| 1.1.2 | Business Plan             | What is the overall quality of the company's business plan?                      |
| 1.1.3 | Other Investors           | Who are the company's existing investors, and who are the potential coinvestors? |
| 1.1.4 | Legal Counsel             | Who is the company's legal counsel?                                              |
| 1.1.5 | Accounting Firm           | Who is the company's accounting firm?                                            |
| 1.1.6 | Origin                    | Does the origin of the company conform to successful patterns?                   |
| 1.1.7 | Customers and/or partners | Has the company signed any impressive customers and/or partners?                 |
| 1.1.8 | Shopped Deal              | Has the deal been shopped around to many investors, each of whom rejected it?    |

## 5. Legal

### 5.1 Pre-investment - General

| #     | Factor     | Question                                                       |

### 5.2 Pre-investment - Intellectual Property (IP)

| #      | Factor                  | Question                                                                                     |
| ------ | ----------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 5.2.1  | General                 | What are the company's IP assets and how important are they to the success of the company?   |
| 5.2.2  | IP competence           | Has the company gained a sufficient understanding of its IP positions and its IP potential?  |
| 5.2.3  | IP development          | Did the company receive government or industry support in development of IP?                 |
| 5.2.4  | IP - Spun out           | Were any of the company's IP assets "spun out" of another company?                           |
| 5.2.5  | Patents                 | Are any of the company's IP assets protected by patent?                                      |
| 5.2.6  | Patent value            | Are the patents valuable to the company?                                                     |
| 5.2.7  | Patent integrity        | Do the patents have sufficient legal integrity?                                              |
| 5.2.8  | Copyrights              | Are any of the company's IP assets protected by copyright?                                   |
| 5.2.9  | Copyright value         | Are the copyrights valuable to the company?                                                  |
| 5.2.10 | Copyright integrity     | Do the copyrights have sufficient legal integrity?                                           |
| 5.2.11 | Trade Secrets           | Are any of the company's IP assets protected by trade secret laws?                           |
| 5.2.12 | Trade Secret value      | Are any of the trade secrets valuable to the company?                                        |
| 5.2.13 | Trade Secret protection | Are structural protections in place to protect the company's trade secrets?                  |
| 5.2.14 | IP licences             | Is the company licencing IP from another company or licencing technology to another company? |
| 5.2.15 | IP Licence value        | If the company is a licensor or licensee of technology, are its licences valuable?           |
| 5.2.16 | IP litigation           | Is the company involved in or vulnerable to any IP material litigation?                      |
| 5.2.17 | Invention assignment    | Are all of the company's founders and employees covered by invention assignment agreements?  |
| 5.2.18 | Non-compete agreements  | Are the company's key employees covered by non-compete agreements?                           |

### 5.3 Pre-investment - Other

| #     | Factor                   | Question                                                                                 |
| ----- | ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 5.3.1 | Existing approval rights | Do any existing investors, those from prior rounds of financing, hold "approval" rights? |
| 5.3.2 | Right of first refusal   | Do any existing investors hold rights of first refusal?                                  |
| 5.3.3 | Anti Dilution            | Do any existing investors hold price-based anti dilution rights?                         |

### 5.4 Transaction - Security

| #      | Factor                   | Question                                                                                                                                                   |
| ------ | ------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 5.4.1  | Security                 | What is the nature of the security to be used for the investment? (Common stock, debt, preferred stock)                                                    |
| 5.4.2  | Conversion rights        | Will the investment security be convertible into another security?                                                                                         |
| 5.4.3  | Conversion terms         | If convertible, what conversion terms will be available?                                                                                                   |
| 5.4.4  | Anti Dilution protection | If convertible, will anti dilution protection for structural changes in the stock be available?                                                            |
| 5.4.5  | Price Base Anti Dilution | If convertible, will anti dilution protection against the issuance of stock at an effective price below conversion be available?                           |
| 5.4.6  | Carve outs               | If price based anti dilution is available, what exceptions is the company seeking for issuances that will not trigger adjustments to the conversion price? |
| 5.4.7  | Pay to Play              | If price based anti dilution is available, will a "pay to play" provision be connected with this protection?                                               |
| 5.4.8  | Dividend preferences     | If the investment is preferred stock, will it have a dividend preference?                                                                                  |
| 5.4.9  | Interest                 | If the investment security is debt, what will be the interest payment terms?                                                                               |
| 5.4.10 | Warrants                 | Will warrants be attached to the investment security?                                                                                                      |
| 5.4.11 | Warrant terms            | If warrants are attached, what terms will be available?                                                                                                    |

### 5.5 Transaction - Control Mechanisms

| #     | Factor                 | Question                                                                                                      |
| ----- | ---------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| 5.5.1 | Board representation   | Will we have representation on the Board of Directors?                                                        |
| 5.5.2 | Extraordinary voting   | Will extraordinary voting rights be available?                                                                |
| 5.5.3 | Board observer         | If Board representation is not available or appropriate, will board observer rights be available?             |
| 5.5.4 | Covenants              | To what covenants will the company be subject? (both positive & negative)                                     |
| 5.5.5 | Repurchase & vesting   | Will the company's management team members be subject to repurchase and vesting provisions?                   |
| 5.5.6 | Staged capital         | Will the commitment of capital to the company be staged in several tranches or closings?                      |
| 5.5.7 | Right of first refusal | Will we have a right of first refusal (pre-emptive right) to participate in future financings of the company? |
| 5.5.8 | Pay to Play            | If given these rights, will a "pay to play" provision be attached to that right?                              |
| 5.5.9 | Redemption rights      | Will redemption rights be triggered if certain milestones are not met?                                        |

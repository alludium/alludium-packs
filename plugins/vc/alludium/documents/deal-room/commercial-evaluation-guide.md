# Commercial Evaluation Guide

## Purpose

Use this guide to evaluate commercial attractiveness before formal diligence. The output should identify whether there is a credible customer segment, urgent pain, budget, business model, and path to adoption.

## Core Questions

| Area | Evaluation Question | Evidence To Seek |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Customer segment | Who is the first narrow customer segment? | Deck, website, call notes, customer examples, pipeline snippets. |
| Pain and urgency | Is the problem painful enough to buy now? | Customer quotes, workflow evidence, budget owner, usage or pilot signals. |
| Budget and pricing | Who pays, how much, and from what budget? | Pricing notes, contracts, LOIs, benchmarks, buyer role. |
| GTM path | How does the company reach customers repeatably? | Sales motion, channels, partner routes, founder network, pipeline. |
| Competition | What alternatives exist and why can this company win? | Competitor list, incumbent/substitute analysis, customer switching logic. |
| Market size | Is the first segment and broader category large enough? | Segmented market estimates, bottom-up logic, public data. |
| Commercial risk | What assumption can most quickly break the case? | Evidence gaps, contradictory signals, weak adoption proof. |

## Output Standard

| Section | Required Content |
| --- | --- |
| Current commercial view | Positive / mixed / negative / unknown. |
| Main commercial thesis | The customer or market belief that must be true. |
| Evidence and confidence | Source-backed evidence with High / Medium / Low confidence. |
| Open proof gaps | What customer, pipeline, pricing, or market evidence is missing. |
| Next questions | Founder, customer, partner, expert, or data-room questions. |
| Decision impact | Whether the opportunity should pass, watch, continue evaluation, or enter targeted diligence. |

## Boundary

Commercial evaluation is not customer validation diligence. Do not state that demand is verified unless customer references, contracts, usage, or equivalent direct evidence have been reviewed.

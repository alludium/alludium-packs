# Lead Generation Packet Template

## Sections

- Meeting objective
- Thesis and search scope
- Candidate company snapshots
- Fit rationale
- Evidence and source links
- Warm intro paths
- Discussion topics
- Proposed next actions
- Open assumptions

## Standard

Make the packet usable for a team review, not just a raw target list. Keep per-company evidence compact and rank the next actions.

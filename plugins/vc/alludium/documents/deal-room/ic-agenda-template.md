# IC Agenda Template

## Agenda

- Decision requested and meeting owner
- Pre-read confirmation
- Company and round overview
- Thesis and fund-fit discussion
- Diligence findings by workstream
- Key risks and dissenting evidence
- Terms, ownership, and reserve implications
- Open questions and conditions
- Decision options
- Follow-up owners and deadlines

## Preparation Rule

The agenda should link the memo, review pack, and decision record. Keep discussion items decision-oriented rather than turning the agenda into a second memo.

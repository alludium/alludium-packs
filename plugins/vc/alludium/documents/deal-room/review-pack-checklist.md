# Review Pack Checklist

## Required Items

- Company snapshot and source context
- Current lifecycle state and decision requested
- Screening scorecard or rationale
- Founder materials status
- Diligence workstream summaries
- Open risks and unresolved questions
- Relevant artifacts and source index
- Recommended next action

## Escalation Rule

Do not escalate a pack as decision-ready if critical evidence is missing. Escalate it as gap review when the purpose is to decide what to investigate next.

# Initial Call Brief Template

## Sections

- Meeting objective
- Company snapshot
- Founder and team context
- Product and customer summary
- Market and competitive context
- Prior evidence and source links
- Starter investment-screen notes
- Questions by topic
- Open risks and unknowns
- Recommended next action

## Standard

Keep the brief short enough to use in the meeting. Label weak evidence clearly and separate pre-call hypotheses from verified facts.

# Paid Source Spend Audit Checklist

## Checks

- Budget owner and limit
- Current spend versus limit
- Query or actor usage spikes
- Cost per usable candidate
- Duplicate or low-quality result share
- Filters that should be tightened
- Sources to pause
- Approval status for expanded usage

## Decision

End each audit with one of continue, reduce, pause, expand, or needs partner review. Include the evidence behind the recommendation.

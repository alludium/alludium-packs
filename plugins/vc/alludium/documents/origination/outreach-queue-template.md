# Outreach Queue Template

## Columns

- Company
- Contact
- Reason to reach out
- Source evidence
- Relationship path
- Suggested channel
- Draft status
- Owner
- Priority
- Approval status
- Last touch
- Next step

## Boundary

This queue prepares outreach. It does not send messages or update external systems unless a later approved task performs that action.

# Origination Source Strategy Guide

## Purpose

Use this guide to operate origination as a standing sourcing system rather than a one-off company search. The goal is to preserve where a candidate came from, why it matters, what evidence exists, what is missing, and whether the candidate is ready for a downstream Deal Room.

## Source Taxonomy

| Source Family | Typical Signals | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Network and referrals | Founder introductions, investor referrals, operator referrals, adviser suggestions. | Capture relationship path and source credibility. |
| Thesis-led outbound | Target lists, category maps, keyword searches, founder research. | Preserve the thesis and why the company matched it. |
| Events and demo days | Accelerator cohorts, pitch events, university demo days, conference lists. | Track event name, date, cohort, and selection quality. |
| University and research channels | Spinouts, technology transfer, research groups, grant-backed ventures. | Track institution, lab, commercial readiness, and IP questions. |
| Data platforms and public sources | Company databases, registries, hiring signals, product launches, open-source activity. | Preserve source freshness, query, run depth, and dedupe state. |
| Inbound and content | Website submissions, email inbound, founder outreach, content-response leads. | Capture founder message, materials, and response deadline. |
| Portfolio and adviser network | Portfolio-company referrals, expert suggestions, advisory-board leads. | Track conflict, strategic relevance, and intro owner. |
| Manual tips | Partner notes, Slack mentions, meeting follow-ups, ad hoc source snippets. | Convert into a structured candidate with provenance before scoring. |

## Source Quality

Every sourced candidate should carry:

| Field | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Source family | The source taxonomy category. |
| Source detail | Specific query, event, list, person, source run, or thread. |
| Owner | Person or task responsible for follow-up. |
| Freshness | When the evidence was collected and whether it is still current. |
| Relationship path | Warm intro, known contact, possible route, or no route. |
| Thesis rationale | Why the candidate matches the configured fund thesis. |
| Evidence quality | High / Medium / Low based on source reliability and completeness. |
| Dedupe state | New, existing, ambiguous, prior pass, or prior watch. |
| Promotion rationale | Why this should or should not enter a Deal Room. |

## Candidate Promotion Standard

Do not promote a candidate using only a name and URL. A promotion package should include:

- company snapshot and source context
- thesis fit and first-look screening result
- source quality and relationship path
- novelty/dedupe assessment
- known risks and missing evidence
- suggested initial Deal Room stage posture
- recommended next task
- required human approval

## Score And Action Bands

| Action | Use When | Required Follow-Up |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Promote | Strong thesis fit, credible source, clear novelty, actionable relationship or evidence path. | Prepare promotion package and request human approval. |
| Review | Attractive but missing source proof, owner judgement, or relationship context. | Assign enrichment, relationship check, or screening. |
| Watch | Relevant but not urgent, too early, too late, or waiting on a trigger. | Define watch trigger, owner, and review date. |
| Reject | Poor fit, stale, duplicate, unsupported, blocked, or already passed. | Record concise rationale and source evidence. |

## Handoff To Deal Room

The Deal Room should inherit the origination trail. Promotion should preserve:

- source provenance
- thesis and category context
- first-look score or verdict
- source artifacts
- relationship path
- unresolved evidence gaps
- recommended starting task

## Standard

Origination outputs should make it clear why the candidate surfaced and what decision the human is being asked to make. Do not create Deal Rooms, mutate CRM records, contact founders, or enable recurring schedules without explicit human approval.
